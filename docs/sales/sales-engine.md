# TripOS Sales Engine

Founder-led sales playbook. You (the founder) close the first ~80 agencies by
hand — no sales hire until ~₹5L MRR. Channel reality: **WhatsApp is the primary
sales channel** for Indian travel-agency owners; email and LinkedIn are support.

Real assets to use everywhere:
- **Founding offer:** 50% off for life, ₹2,499 (Starter) / ₹4,999 (Pro), real cap 100, closes **31 Jul 2026**. Genuine scarcity — use it, don't fake it.
- **Free trial:** 14 days, no card.
- **Live proof:** Devendra Sharma, Owner, Bhagwati Holidays, Jodhpur (see `reviews.ts`).
- **Competitor positioning:** see `docs/sales/battle-cards.md`.
- **Review triggers:** every close feeds `docs/sales/review-capture-system.md`.

## 1. Buyer personas

### Persona A — "Owner-Operator" (PRIMARY, ~70% of effort)
- **Who:** Owns a 1–5 person agency, tier-1/2 city, sells outbound packages (Bali, Dubai, Thailand, Europe, Vietnam, Maldives) + domestic (Kashmir, Kerala, Rajasthan, NE).
- **Stack today:** WhatsApp + Excel + Canva + Word; invoices via CA or Tally.
- **Pain:** Proposals take hours and look ordinary; leads slip; manual GST; no idea what's converting.
- **Buys on:** looking professional, speed, "one extra booking pays for it." Owner decides in 1–2 conversations.
- **Where to find:** Instagram travel-agent hashtags, Facebook agent groups, IATA/TAAI member lists, B2B DMC WhatsApp groups, local agency associations.

### Persona B — "Solo Travelpreneur" (VOLUME, ~20%)
- **Who:** Ex-corporate / influencer-style solo consultant selling on Instagram.
- **Pain:** Needs to look like a real brand; no back office.
- **Buys on:** branding + ease; lowest ARPU, highest referral velocity. Starter plan.

### Persona C — "Growing Agency / small DMC" (ARPU, ~10%)
- **Who:** 6–20 people, multiple agents, quoting at volume.
- **Pain:** No visibility into agents/leads/margins; chaos as team grows.
- **Buys on:** pipeline, agent analytics, multi-seat. Pro / Scale; longer cycle (2–4 touches).

## 6. Demo script (15 minutes — the core close)

1. **(1 min) Anchor the pain:** "Before I show anything — walk me through what happens from the moment a {{Bali}} enquiry hits your WhatsApp today." (Let them describe Excel/Word/Canva. Take notes; mirror their words later.)
2. **(5 min) The wow:** Build *their* enquiry live → type brief → AI itinerary → apply their branding → send on WhatsApp. "That's the 3 hours you just described, in 8 minutes."
3. **(3 min) Never lose a lead:** Show the pipeline + follow-up reminders/automation. "This is why agencies stop losing enquiries between WhatsApp chats."
4. **(3 min) The India trust closer:** Generate a GST tax invoice + collect a test payment. "Correct tax split, sequential numbering, INR — your CA is happy, your client can pay instantly."
5. **(3 min) Close:** "So — proposal time from hours to minutes, no lost leads, GST sorted. Founding price is 50% off for life and closes 31 July. Let's start your free trial now and I'll import your hot leads while we're on the call." → **Start the trial on the call. Do not 'send a link to sign up later.'**

**Always:** start the trial and do the first real action (import leads / build one proposal) *during* the call. Activation = conversion.

## 7. Trial activation playbook (Day 0–3 — the make-or-break window)

Goal: get them to the **"aha" = first branded proposal sent on WhatsApp** within 48 hours. Activated trials convert; un-activated ones churn.

- **Day 0 (on the call or within 1 hr):** Account live, logo + agency details added, ONE real proposal built and sent together. This is the single highest-leverage action in the whole funnel.
- **Day 0 (WhatsApp):** "You're all set 🎉 Here's your account + the proposal we just built. Reply here anytime — I personally read these."
- **Day 1:** "Did the proposal land okay with your client? Want me to import your other open leads so they're all in one place?"
- **Day 2 (if not activated):** "Noticed you haven't sent a proposal yet — give me 10 minutes and I'll build one with you on a real enquiry. When suits?"
- **Day 3:** Share one quick tip relevant to their work (e.g. "here's how to clone that Bali itinerary for the next client").

**Activation checklist (track per trial):** logo added · 1 AI itinerary generated · 1 proposal sent on WhatsApp · 1 lead imported. 3 of 4 = likely to convert.

## 8. Trial conversion playbook (Day 4–14)

- **Day 5:** "How's it feeling vs your old Word/Canva flow? Anything clunky — tell me, I'll fix it fast." (Surface objections early.)
- **Day 7 (midpoint):** Send Devendra's quote + a relevant capability ("agents like you use the GST invoice + payment link together — want me to show you?").
- **Day 10:** Value recap in their numbers: "You've sent {{N}} proposals and saved ~{{N×2}} hours this week."
- **Day 11:** "3 days left. After the trial you'd lose {{Pro features/team seats}}. Lock the founding price (50% for life) now — it closes 31 July regardless."
- **Day 13:** "Last day tomorrow — want me to switch you to {{plan}} so nothing pauses? Takes 1 minute." Offer to get on a call to do it together.
- **Day 14:** Founding scarcity + a direct "shall I activate {{plan}} now?" with the payment link.

**Conversion rule:** ask for the sale explicitly at least twice (Day 11 + Day 14). Most trials convert because you asked, not because they decided alone.

## 9. Churn recovery playbook

**At cancellation (in-app + WhatsApp within 1 hr):**
> "Sorry to see you go {{Name}} 🙏 Genuinely helps me — what's the one thing that made it not work? (Too busy to set up / missing a feature / price / something else?)"

Branch by reason:
- **Didn't activate / too busy:** "Totally fair — most of the value comes after the first proposal, which we never finished. Give me 15 minutes this week and I'll set it all up *for* you. If it's still not worth it, no charge." → win-back trial extension.
- **Missing feature:** Log it. "We ship weekly — I'll personally tell you the day it's live. Can I keep your data warm so you don't restart?"
- **Price:** "I can hold the founding rate (50% for life) for you. Would {{Starter}} work better than {{Pro}} for now?"
- **Switched to competitor:** "No hard feelings — what won it for them? (Genuinely want to know.)" Log competitive intel into battle cards.

**Dormant win-back (30/60 days later):** "Hi {{Name}} — we shipped {{the thing you wanted}}. Want to pick up where you left off? Your data's still here and I can re-open your founding rate."

## 10. Referral system

Travel-agency owners trust other owners far more than ads. Make referrals the cheapest channel.

- **In-app mechanic (build — Wave 6 product):** "Refer an agent — you both get 1 month free." Unique link, tracked.
- **Ask every happy customer (script):** "Glad it's working 🙏 Most agents I onboard come from another agent's recommendation — is there one person you'd point me to? Happy to give you both a free month."
- **Founding-cohort multiplier:** each of the 38 → ask for 2 intros + 1 public review (see review-capture system). That alone seeds the next ~75 agencies and your review count.
- **Trainer/educator affiliates (Wave: partnerships):** revenue-share for "start a travel agency" course sellers who bundle tripOS.
- **Community referral leaderboard:** run it inside your free "Travel Agency Growth (India)" WhatsApp/Telegram group.

**Referral close rate beats cold by ~5–10×. Treat every onboarding as the start of the next two.**
