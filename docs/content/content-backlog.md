## Operating cadence
- **2 posts/week.** Alternate: 1 high-volume top-funnel (traffic) + 1 bottom-funnel/destination (conversion).
- Every post: internal-link to ≥1 SEO landing page + ≥1 other post, and end with a `cta` block.
- Every destination playbook follows the Bali template (segment → sample itinerary → pricing → close) — it's a repeatable factory.

🟢 High (buyer/transactional) · 🟡 Medium (commercial/comparison) · ⚪ Low (top-funnel traffic/authority)
