## 1. The problem you solve (say it in THEIR words)
Pick the 1–2 that land. Watch them nod.
- **"Proposals eat half your day** in Word/Canva — and still look generic."
- **"Leads slip"** across WhatsApp chats, notebooks, and memory.
- **"You chase payments by hand"** and dread GST season.
- **"You have no idea** which agent, source, or month actually makes money."
- **"Everything lives in 5 different tools** that don't talk to each other."

> Frame: *"You're doing the work — you're just losing hours (and deals) to the tools. tripOS is the one place that runs the whole thing."*

## 3. Discovery questions to ASK (this is the heart of the call)
Ask, then **shut up and listen**. Their answers tell you exactly what to pitch.
Aim for ~70% them talking. Jot their numbers — you'll quote them back at close.

### A. Warm-up (build rapport, get context)
- "Tell me about your agency — what kind of trips do you mostly do? *(honeymoon / family / pilgrimage / group / international FIT?)*"
- "How big is the team — just you, or a few agents?"
- "Roughly how many enquiries do you get a week? How many turn into bookings?"

### B. Pain discovery (make them feel the problem)
**Proposals:**
- "Walk me through what happens when a client enquires — what do you do first?"
- **"How long does one proposal take you to build right now?"** *(listen for "2–3 hours / half a day")*
- "What do you build it in — Word, Canva, PDF? Does it look the way you'd like?"

**Leads / follow-up:**
- "Where do your leads live — WhatsApp, a diary, your head?"
- "Ever lost a booking because a follow-up slipped?"

**Payments & GST:**
- "How do you collect advance payments today? How much chasing is involved?"
- **"How do you handle GST invoices?"** *(listen for "my CA does it / Excel / it's a headache")*

**Visibility:**
- "Do you know which agent or which source (Insta, referral, etc.) brings your best bookings?"
- "At month-end, can you see your actual margin per trip?"

### C. Qualify (are they a fit? who decides?)
- "What tools are you using now — anything, or mostly manual?"
- "If something solved this, who'd be involved in deciding — just you?"
- "Have you tried any travel software before? What made you stop?"

### D. Set up the close (create urgency)
- "If you could send a proposal in 10 minutes instead of 3 hours, how many more would you actually send a week?"
- "What would closing even 1–2 extra bookings a month be worth to you?"

## 5. The mini live-demo (trigger the "aha" — use THEIR real trip)
Only if you can screen-share. 5–10 min. Use a trip they're actually quoting.
1. **"Give me a destination and rough brief."** → generate the day-by-day itinerary. *"That took 10 seconds. How long in Word?"*
2. Edit one day, swap a hotel → *"Fully yours to shape."*
3. Add quote line items + markup → show **cost / selling / profit** → *"You see your margin; the client never will."*
4. Send the branded proposal to **your own** WhatsApp → open on phone → tap **Accept** → it flips to a booking.
5. Generate a **payment link**, then a **one-click GST invoice.**
6. Flash the **Reports** screen → *"This is the money view."*
7. **"Want me to set this up on your real trips? I'll start your trial right now."**
